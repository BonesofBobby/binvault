# BinVault Product Roadmap

> Know what you own. Know where it is.

---

# Current Vision

BinVault is a self-hosted household inventory and information platform.

Rather than focusing only on storage containers, BinVault is designed to become the central knowledge base for everything a homeowner owns and maintains.

---
