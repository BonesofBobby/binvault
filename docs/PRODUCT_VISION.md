# BinVault Product Vision

Version: 1.0

Last Updated: July 2026

---

# Vision

BinVault is a modern home inventory and asset management platform designed to help individuals and families organize, locate, maintain, and protect the things they own.

Rather than acting as a simple inventory database, BinVault serves as a Home Operations Platform that provides visibility into physical possessions, important documents, warranties, maintenance schedules, storage locations, and household assets.

The long-term goal is to reduce time spent searching for items, simplify home management, improve disaster preparedness, and preserve valuable information about owned property.

---

# Mission

Create the most intuitive, reliable, and extensible home inventory platform available while demonstrating production-quality software engineering practices.

BinVault should feel equally appropriate for:

- Homeowners
- Families
- Collectors
- Hobbyists
- Small businesses
- Rental property owners
- Insurance documentation
- Emergency preparedness

---

# Product Principles

## 1. Simplicity

Users should never feel overwhelmed.

Common tasks should require only a few clicks.

---

## 2. Organization First

Everything should have a logical place.

Items belong inside containers.

Containers belong inside locations.

Locations belong inside properties.

---

## 3. Search Everything

Users should be able to locate any possession within seconds.

Search should eventually include:

- Item names
- Categories
- Containers
- Locations
- Manufacturers
- Tags
- Documents
- Photos
- QR codes

---

## 4. Rich Asset History

Every important object should have a complete history.

Examples include:

- Purchase information
- Warranty
- Receipt
- Photos
- Manuals
- Maintenance history
- Estimated value
- Notes

---

## 5. Actionable Intelligence

BinVault should proactively surface useful information.

Examples include:

- Maintenance reminders
- Expiring warranties
- Expiring documents
- Missing photos
- Low inventory
- Storage utilization
- Household insights

---

## 6. Mobile Friendly

The application should work seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Future releases should support QR code scanning directly from a mobile phone.

---

## 7. AI Assisted

Artificial intelligence should enhance—not replace—the user experience.

Potential AI features include:

- Natural language search
- Receipt extraction
- Automatic categorization
- Duplicate detection
- Maintenance suggestions
- Household insights

---

# Long-Term Vision

BinVault will evolve into a comprehensive digital record of the home.

It should answer questions such as:

- Where is my passport?
- Which smoke detector battery needs replacing?
- Which warranties expire this month?
- Where are the Christmas decorations stored?
- What is everything I own from Milwaukee?
- What should I take if I need to evacuate?

---

# Success Metrics

A successful BinVault implementation enables users to:

- Find items quickly
- Track valuable assets
- Reduce duplicate purchases
- Improve household organization
- Prepare for insurance claims
- Simplify maintenance planning
- Increase confidence during emergencies
