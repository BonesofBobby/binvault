# BinVault

## Household Inventory & Information Platform

---

## Mission

BinVault helps homeowners know what they own, where it is stored, how to maintain it, and how to find it instantly.

The platform combines household inventory, storage management, document organization, maintenance tracking, QR code labeling, and intelligent search into one self-hosted application.

---

# Vision

Every household should have a searchable digital inventory.

Instead of searching through bins, cabinets, drawers, filing cabinets, spreadsheets, and cloud storage...

Search BinVault.

---

# Core Principles

## Inventory First

Inventory is the center of the system.

Containers, locations, documents, assets, maintenance records, and QR codes all exist to organize and enrich inventory.

---

## Search First

Users should never need to remember where something is stored.

The system should tell them.

---

## Offline First

BinVault is designed to operate completely offline.

Users own their data.

---

## Family Friendly

Every member of the household should be able to use BinVault without technical knowledge.

---

## Long-Term Ownership

BinVault is intended to remain useful for decades.

The application should grow with the household.

---

# Target Users

• Homeowners

• Hobbyists

• Preparedness enthusiasts

---

# Product Goals

• Reduce duplicate purchases

• Improve organization

• Preserve important information

• Track household assets

• Simplify maintenance

• Provide instant retrieval of information

---

# Non Goals

BinVault is not intended to replace:

• Accounting software

• ERP systems

• Warehouse management systems

• Retail inventory systems

BinVault focuses exclusively on household information management.
